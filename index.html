What Is Sgara?

Sgara is a data-driven farm advisory and marketplace solutions for Shrimp farmers. With AI, we interpret real-time data inputs from farms and provide insightful solutions. Assisting farmers with our end-to-end services such as farm management and access to suppliers nationwide.
